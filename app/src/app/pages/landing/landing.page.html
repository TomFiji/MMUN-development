Benefits to You…
If you have a humanitarian project in place, in development or planned, Hear Me Now! is your opportunity to showcase it, develop a following, increase funding opportunities and gain further opportunities as an HMN! Project Nominee or HMN! Award Winner©

What You Need to Do…
Register yourself and post your project Activate your Donate Now button Ask your network to view your project and vote for it.

What We Will Do…
Projects with 50 or more votes will be evaluated by our panelists and if selected, will receive special recognition on the HMN! site as an HMN! Award Nominee © and we will help promote and drive attention to your project. HMN! Award Winners © will receive even greater support and their projects will be showcased to potential donors.

Special programming for educators

OUR RESPONSIBILITIES TO YOU



To magnify the voice of youth and young adults in the halls of government and corporate boardrooms by bringing attention to how they vote on issues that concern them and why they think that way about the issues. Vote tabulations will be provided to the media and submitted to government and corporate leaders. The votes you cast today are indicators of how you will vote in the future, and that carries weight with corporate and government officials.

Another responsibility is to provide a global platform for youth and young adults that will make it easier and safer for them to engage with others in forums, and to develop support for their projects through participation and funding.

Benefits to you:

  • #
    Yours and the votes of others help make your voice heard because we will use media and our well-connected contacts to place your votes and your reasoning in front of decision makers and influencers.
  • #
    An easy to use platform for growing your community and gain support for your projects. We are here to help you make this a better world.
  • #
    Special, age-specific networking activities.
  • #
    Opportunities for advancement within your community.
  • #
    Opportunities to raise funds for your project.
  • #
    Opportunities for advancement within the Hear Me Now! universe as a HMN! Local Leader.
  • #
    We are representing you in the halls of government and corporate boardrooms.

And finally…we’ll help you have fun as you achieve your goals!

For youth age 9-12 and 12-15

Vote on the issues that concern you, create your own cause or help support the causes of others, participate in important surveys, encourage others to join with you in supporting a cause and enjoy the opportunity to make new friends around the world.
